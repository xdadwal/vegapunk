Hey team! Here's the plan for a laid-back monsoon weekend in Kasauli — pine forests, misty hills, easy walks, and a rooftop bar with live music. We leave Friday night, get two full days in the hills, and roll back Monday afternoon. Read through and let's lock it in.

🌿The Vibe

Kasauli is a quiet British-era hill station at ~6,000 ft in the Shivalik hills. July is off-season monsoon: green, misty, uncrowded, and cheaper. Expect ~24–30°C, occasional showers, and cool evenings. Think slow walks, chai with a view, and no packed tourist crowds.

🎒What to Pack

  • Light jacket / hoodie (evenings get cool)
  • Umbrella or rain shell (monsoon showers)
  • Comfortable walking shoes with grip (trails can be damp)
  • A book / cards for lazy cafe afternoons

🗓️The Itinerary

Friday Night · 10 Jul

Hit the Road

  • Leave Delhi late evening after work / dinner on the way.
  • Overnight drive with chai-and-snack breaks; reach Kasauli by early morning.
Day 1 · Saturday 11 Jul

Settle & Stroll

  • Early check-in, freshen up, and a relaxed breakfast to recover from the drive.
  • Lazy morning — nap or coffee on the terrace.
  • Evening walk on The Mall Road & Upper Mall — cool air, pine forest, no traffic.
  • Sunset at Sunset Point / Lover's Lane — easy, quiet forest walk.
  • Dinner + live music at the Hangout Rooftop Bar (at our hotel).
Day 2 · Sunday 12 Jul

Views & Easy Sights

  • Morning: Christ Church and a slow amble around the colonial town.
  • Gilbert Trail — flat, scenic 1.5 km nature walk, great for birds and mist views.
  • Optional detour: Kasauli Brewery area / Baba Balak Nath viewpoint.
  • Sunday is Open Mic night at Hangout — a great last evening.
Day 3 · Monday 13 Jul

Slow Morning & Head Back

  • Relaxed breakfast, last walk, pick up local souvenirs (jams, honey, cheese).
  • Check out and start back after an early lunch — reach Delhi by evening.

Tip: Keep it unhurried — Kasauli's charm is in slow walks and chai with a view. Optional extension: a Sanawar or Chail add-on if we have the time.
